Overview

Predict left / center / right using pre-kick body motion only. We extract a 17-point skeleton across 30 frames (3–5s pre-kick), normalize, and feed to an LSTM trained to anticipate direction before contact.

Method

  • 560 broadcast clips (2015–2024), 3–5s windows
  • YOLOv8 detection + pose, 17 keypoints × 30 frames
  • LSTM over 34 coordinates (x,y) × 30 time-steps
  • Held-out evaluation, joint importance inspection
